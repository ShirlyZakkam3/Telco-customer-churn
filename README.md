# 📊 Telco Customer Churn — Exploratory Analytics

**Domain:** Telecommunications — Customer Retention & Churn Analysis  
**Tool:** Tableau  
**Dataset:** [Telco Customer Churn — Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn)

---

## Problem Statement

Telecommunication providers face increasing customer churn but often lack clear, data-driven insight into when customers leave, which segments are most at risk, and what factors drive churn. Without a structured exploratory analysis of customer behaviour, contract types, pricing, and service usage, retention decisions are reactive and inefficient.

This project visually explores and understands churn drivers to support informed, evidence-based customer retention strategies.

---

## Dataset

| Property | Detail |
|---|---|
| Source | Kaggle (widely used, structured, reliable) |
| Records | 7,043 customers |
| Features | Demographics, services, billing, churn status |

---

## Key Questions Explored

- Which customers are more likely to churn?
- How do tenure and contract type affect churn?
- How do pricing, billing methods, and service usage influence churn?
- Where is churn most concentrated across the customer lifecycle?

---

## Key Insights

- Churn is highest among **short-tenure customers**
- **Month-to-month contracts** have significantly higher churn rates
- Customers using **electronic check** payment show higher churn risk
- **Higher monthly charges** increase churn, especially early in the lifecycle
- Customers with **multiple services and support features** are more loyal
- Churn can still occur among high-value customers, leading to revenue loss

## Dashboards Built

| Dashboard | Focus |
|---|---|
| Executive Overview | Overall churn rate, contract type, gender distribution |
| Business Insights | Monthly charges, payment method, tenure groups, internet service |
| Pricing & Contract Risk | Contract × payment method matrix, monthly charges distribution |
| Customer Behaviour & Risk | Risk segmentation, feature adoption, tech support impact |
| Customer Lifecycle & Billing | Lifecycle stages, paperless billing, stability index |
| Contract Value & Risk | Customer flow, churn heat map, revenue at risk |
| Loyalty & Long-Term Value | Individual services, payment risk profile, service dependency |
| Tenure & Value Risk Analytics | Churn acceleration, lifetime value risk bands, probability curve |
| Digital Entertainment & Streaming | Streaming behaviour, digital service adoption, security impact |
| Customer Commitment vs Churn Shift | Silent revenue leakage, commitment vs churn, digital dependency paradox |

---

## Actionable Insights

- Prioritise **early-stage customers** for retention — churn is highest in year one
- Encourage migration from **month-to-month to long-term contracts**
- Review **pricing strategies** for new customers, especially those with higher monthly charges
- Promote **automatic payment methods** to reduce churn linked to billing friction
- Increase adoption of **support and security services**, which are associated with stronger customer retention
- Monitor **high-value customers** closely, as their churn leads to significant revenue loss
